Digital Marketing Job Opportunities

Once you have done with the digital marketing course, you will easily be able to get digital marketing jobs in various modules like SEO, SMM, Google Adwords, Web Analytics, Content Marketer, etc.

Nowadays, there are many digital marketing agencies and companies that are looking for digital marketing professionals from various fields.

Now, let’s look into the various digital marketing job opportunities with their salaries that are paid to the professionals in the field of Digital Marketing.

 

  • Digital Marketing Manager 

The very first job opportunity that you can get in the field of digital marketing is as a Digital Marketing Manager where your main job role will be focused on the planning, development, implementation, and management of the overall company digital strategy.

Once you have got into the job role of a digital marketing manager you can easily earn an annual salary of approx Rs. 4.7 lacs.

  • SEO Specialists

As reported by SEOTribunal.com, Google handles 3.8 million searches per minute on an average across the globe.

Therefore, from the above stats, we can see that with the continuous increase in the daily search the demand for SEO specialists is also increasing day by day.

So as an SEO Specialist you will be required to analyze, suggest and implement the organization’s website so that it can be optimized for the search engines so that it can be easily ranked. Also, you will be required to implement keyword research, On-page SEO, Off- Page and Technical SEO where you will be able to get a minimum salary range from Rs. 1LPA to Rs. 4 LPA and for an annual salary of an experienced candidate will range from Rs. 1.9 Lacs.

  • Social Media Marketer

As most people are using social media platforms these days, the job responsibilities of social media marketer revolve around creating content, promoting products and services, scheduling social media posts, interacting with customers, developing new social media strategies, and running marketing campaigns over various social media platforms like Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram, and Snapchat.

So, if you begin your career as a Social Media Marketer, you will easily be able to earn a starting average salary of Rs. 3. 5 LPA.

  • Web Analyst/Data Analyst

Web Analysts and Data Analysts are part of the Web Development team. The main responsibilities of these professionals are data management, data analysis, web projects, data integrity, and web server security.

The salary range of a Web Analyst/Data Analyst is ₹1.8–6.6 LPA. The average salary of an experienced candidate is ₹3.6 LPA.

  • Content Marketer

As a Content Marketer, you will be responsible for creating various content like blog posts, social media posts, guest posts, etc to engage your targeted audience.

The salary offered to you will range from Rs. 3.7 LPA to Rs. 10 LPA. Once you have acquired enough experience in the field of content writing and marketing you will get an annual salary of around Rs. 6.4 Lacs.
