Behind Every Successful Entrepreneur is a Mother!

Being an entrepreneur is hard, being a mother is tougher.
The prominent online publication – BW Disrupt – has featured Digiperform on the occasion of Mother’s Day among industry players like UrbanClap, Momspresso, Salebhai etc., on 13th May 2018.
The publication has carried the industry story by Manu Jolly, Co-Founder of Digiperform under the headline,”Behind every successful Entrepreneur is a Mother“.
​ ​
The story highlights the success mantra of renowned entrepreneurs and 1 piece of advice given by their mothers that is helping them in their personal & professional lives.
