Various Digital Marketing Job opportunities in South Delhi

 

1. Digital Marketing Manager

The very first job opportunity that you can get as a digital marketing professional, is to become a digital marketing manager in one of the reputed companies or the MNC where you will have the responsibility to strategize and implement the complete digital marketing campaign strategy of an organization to build a strong brand reputation and promote the various products and services to get in front of the targeted audience.

Besides this, you will be also responsible for leading the entire marketing team and reporting to the head of marketing or VP of digital marketing of the company.
Therefore, to become a digital marketing manager, you should have experience of more than 5+ years in the same industry to back up your expertise as a digital marketer.

Digital Marketing Manager Jobs in South Delhi 

If you are passionate enough about digital marketing, then you can easily work as a Digital Marketing Manager. 

2. SEO Expert

Once you have completed the digital marketing course, you can easily apply for the job role as a Search Engine Optimization Expert.

Here, you will have the responsibility to rank the website on the Search Engine Resulting Page.

Not only you will be responsible for ranking the website on the top of SERPs, but also be responsible for analyzing, reviewing, and optimizing the website using the various SEO techniques based on the latest trends and news on the different search engines like Google, Bing, Yahoo, etc.

SEO Expert job in South Delhi

3. Social Media Marking

As a Social Media Marketer, you will have the responsibility of handling the various social media platforms like Facebook, Twitter, Linkedin, Youtube, and Pinterest, etc of the company to design & execute the complete social media strategy of the company.

Also, you will have the opportunity of creating content, including text posts, video, and images for promoting the company products, services, and content over various social media platforms and measuring some of the key metrics like reach, impressions, and engagement of the customers and convert them into the actual customers.

SMM Expert job in South Delhi

4. Content Marketer

In the digital marketing industry, content is king. As a content marketer of a company, you will be responsible for creating the content for a Company blog where it can be in the form of articles, videos, email newsletters, social media, etc.

The main aim that you will have as a content marketer will be to create valuable content and distribute it among the various relevant channels to attract the targeted audience as well as to increase the website traffic.

Also, you will have to work on various tasks like managing a blog, creating various campaigns, copywriting, guest blogging, PR, and video creation.

Content Marketing Job in South Delhi

 

PPC Expert

As a PPC Expert, you will be responsible for handling the paid ads of the various platforms like search engines, social media platforms to attract customers and increase the brand awareness of the company with the help of ads running on various platforms.

With this, the job role of the PPC Expert will also be to plan, create and manage the PPC campaigns across a variety of platforms, do proper keyword research  & management skills to optimize the bids at the keyword, placement, device, geographic, demographic, etc including the monitoring, evaluation, and presenting the performance of campaigns.

By generating the weekly or monthly reports to track the performance base on analyzing the trends, goals, opportunities, and other key performance indicators

PPC Expert job in South Delhi
