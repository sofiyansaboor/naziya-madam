]]> Digiperform is delighted to announce that we are spreading our arms all over the eastern region of India by launching 31st center in Kolkata (also known as Calcutta), West Bengal. Kolkata is also nicknamed as “City of Joy” and regarded as the “Cultural Capital of India”. And, According to Wikipedia, Kolkata is densely populated with a total population of 14.1 million which makes it the third most populous metropolitan city in India. With this huge population density, Our 31st center is all ready to impart digital marketing training and empower them with the most demanding skill sets of this millennium. As digitalization is at its par, getting skilled in digital marketing will help freshers to start their career in the most demanding field. Also, it will help entrepreneurs or wannabe be entrepreneurs to understand the role of digital platforms that can play in any business success. Being a true devotee of his land, Mr. Faizan Rahman, Digiperform Partner in Kolkata, has launched digital marketing center in Kolkata on 05th Jan 2019 with a pledge to educate the young population of Kolkata about digital marketing and help them to grab the number of opportunities for a better future.

About Digiperform:

Digiperform India’s Most Trusted Brand in Digital Marketing Training who believes to impart digital marketing skills to all everyone irrespective of their degrees, classes, organization levels, and gender. Digiperform provides two major certification training in online marketing i.e. dCDME and dCDMC to fresh graduates, working professionals, and wannabe entrepreneurs, with a motto to enable them to be self-employed get placed in top organizations & understand the operation of Digital Marketing to run their own empires.
