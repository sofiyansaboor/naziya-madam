When making backlinks and performing link building to boost your business site’s SEO, knowing how to optimally approach the task is essential. Without the right knowledge, doing so can become a highly intimidating and demanding task.

Educating yourself on this issue is the best way to ensure your website has an extremely large online presence, which will then boost your SEO. To get you started, here are 10 important tactics to use when performing manual link building:

1. Ask People for Backlinks

If you do not ask people to backlink to your site, you’re approaching SEO completely incorrectly. Not only can this benefit you, but it can help you build relationships with these sites. Offer to post backlinks for them as well, and your chances will increase. Ask for links that are in the content, and seek out sites that are relevant to your business’s offerings. 

2. List Your Site in Quality Directories

Directories are still widely used on the internet. When people are looking for a specific product or service, they often seek out directories that list relevant businesses. Look for local directories, and other wider-scoped directories, and begin placing your business’s site on them, and you will create useful, SEO-building backlinks quickly.

If you’re not taking advantage of directories, you are making a serious SEO mistake. 

3. Guest Posts

Bloggers are not always on top of their next article idea. If you can find a blog that discusses topics relevant to your business, you can write guest posts for them that will then drive qualified customers to your website through backlinks.

Just ensure the site is relevant to your business, that the article is not written as a visible soft-sell for your business, and that the content is amazingly written, and you’ll be good to go. 

4. Start Turning Your Mentions Into Backlinks

You need to know when to best leverage your existing opportunities. Other people have likely mentioned or discussed your business online, after all. By reaching out to the webmaster or site creator where this is occurring, you may get them to add backlinks to the content mentioning your website.

Doing so is a great way to cleverly boost the number of backlinks that are boosting your SEO. 

5. Begin a Blog

Blogs are perfect for creating content that will reel readers into topics that are relevant to your business. Placing the right keywords in these blog posts, and then backlinking through them, will boost your website’s traffic significantly.

As long as your content is useful, specific, and well-written, it will do wonders for boosting your business’s authority within your industry. 

6. Search Out Backlink You Have in Common with Your Competitors

While researching your competitors, you may find that they have similar links they’re making use of. Using online tools that can help you analyze the backlinks of your competitors is the key here. Using manual link building services can be especially useful, so be sure to seriously consider it. 

7. Repairing Dead Backlinks

Sometimes links are broken, or websites become dysfunctional. Searching out your existing backlinks, and making sure they are active is crucial. There are online tools that will help you do just this. With the right amount of time put into this task, you’ll optimize your backlinks in a major way. 

8. Write Testimonials

Testimonials will help you emphasize your connection to your local community. Write testimonials for products or services you’ve recently loved, and you’ll help link people back to your site. If a product or service is especially related to your business, the testimonial effort will be much more likely to lead to increased online visibility and SEO. 

9. Always Build Relationships

Despite what business you might be in, networking and connecting with others is crucial to your success. This will allow you to reach out to others in a much more effective fashion.

Begin your search with niche communities related to your business, and continue looking for other valuable relationships that will help boost the visibility of your business (often through backlinks). 

10. Seek Out the Best Link Building Opportunities Possible

Whether it be site links, blogs, forums, or directories, they’re are always backlink opportunities out there for you to seek out. To use the rest of these tips effectively, this mindset is an absolute necessity. Use research tools, hard work ethic, and dedication to nail your search. 

Optimize Your Efforts

With these ten tactics, you’ll be fully prepared to make your site’s SEO completely optimized. Link building, backlinks, and other SEO boosting tactics, your business will be more visible online than ever before. The process can seem intimidating, but many businesses hire SEO experts to get the job done.

Here you need to know both the on-page and off-page SEO tactics to ensure that your website gains more online awareness, brand recall, experiences better sales and also increases its customer base.

What is On-Page & Off-Page SEO?

On-page SEO is one of the SEO practices of optimizing individual webpages in order to rank higher and earn more relevant traffic in search engines. It refers to both the content and HTML source code of a page that can be optimized, as opposed to off-page SEO which refers to links and other external signals. Whereas, Off-page SEO refers to techniques that can be used to improve the position of a web site in the search engine results page (SERPs). Many people associate off–page SEO with link building but it is not only that. Do you resonate with the same? If yes, then you might as well depend on the best on-page and off-page SEO techniques to maximize the organic traffic and online visibility.

The best five on-page SEO tactics

Simply put, as the term suggests, the on-page tactics are all to do with initiatives which get implemented on your web page. The five best tactics to refer to and include the following:

  • Quality content is an essential aspect

You should curate high-quality blog and website visual content. The ideas you want to write on should be interesting and original. Address the customer pain points using information and draw meaningful references. It will strike a chord with your customers and make them want to read more.

  • Always link with quality sites

Links sourced from high-authority websites are essential. They always alert the search engine that your blog or articles published on your website are reliable. Some of the best examples of high authority sites include CNN, Huffington Post, Forbes, Drudge and many more.

  • Opt-in for the Dublin Core Metadata components

Search engines give sufficient importance to keywords in the Metadata. The majority of experts suggest incorporating keywords to the Metadata. The keywords also need to match all through the Metadata from the description to the title. That way it’s not penalized. Here it’s essential to make use of the Dublin Core Metadata. It is an open-source platform that got created to enable SEO experts curate content that can get discovered on the semantic web. 

  • Delete the old blog posts and content

Google algorithms are always changing. It also propels SEO to change along with it. Your content should always reflect the algorithm changes. Hence, it’s a smart call to delete all your old posts and content. Alternatively, you can remove these posts from the site or blog, tweak them according to the new SEO trends and re-upload them again. That way you will have space for new content.

  • Publish long-form content

The new age online user and viewer is cyber savvy. They know what they are searching for. Also, people, today want facts and exciting perspectives. They are looking for informative and entertaining posts that can solve their queries or help them learn something new. All isn’t possible if you are curating a short-content piece. Hence, it’s a smart call to write long content that elucidates various perspectives and incorporates multiple facts. Anything between 2000 and 3000 words count is a good choice. 

The best five on-page SEO tactics

Simply put the off-page SEO is all about optimizing a brand’s offline and online footprint by using the content.   • Leverage social media platforms

There’s more to social media than just informal and casual talks. Today, the majority of brands have social media profiles where they share their company updates, brand launches, online offers, interesting company developments, news about mergers and acquisitions and the like. Social media helps brands to develop a personal dialogue with customers, which also helps to increase the customer base and organic traffic.

  • Blog marketing

Do you have a blog where you post regularly? If yes, then you must have come across comments on your posts, with relevant blog links that either matches your blog post or your industry vertical. It is known as blog marketing. And even you can do this for your brand. As you read another interesting blog post, leave a meaningful reply or information and link it to your blog. It will help people know about your brand and blog. Both your website and blog will gain organic traffic.

  • Try out forum marketing

Here you need to browse online and come across forums that are associated with your niche. Once you do that, start getting engaged in the community. You can start by replying to interesting conversation threads, take time to answer various questions, share your suggestions and many more. It is useful in developing a good online reputation for your brand. Gradually you get the tag of an expert within your industry vertical. Here you need to make use of the “Do Follow” forums. It will enable you to add in a link if your company site and add a signature. The search engines will then crawl up the site.

  • Opt-in for directory submission

Directory submission isn’t obsolete! The task is to use the directories and leverage the benefits effectively. Also, a lot depends on how a brand chooses the submission category in the directory. You might select the general directories. However, if you want the best outcomes and gain organic traffic, it’s a smart call to submit in the niche directories. It does take some time to show the results, but consistent effort always counts. So go all ahead with your Google My Business and Yelp directories today.

  • Don’t forget the power of social bookmarking

Social Bookmarking is an important off-page SEO tactic that small businesses tend to overlook. Done effectively it is useful in website promotion. Here you will have to submit a recent web page or blog post to the best social bookmarking sites. You could use names like Delicious, StumbleUpon, Reddit, Digg and many more. Search engines prefer similar sites as the content gets updated regularly. It is essential to manage the tags in social bookmarking smartly. As it can impact the way, you broadcast news in a broad network. It is applicable for small, medium and big scale businesses. Hence, make a start today, and you can add more useful methods as your company expands.
