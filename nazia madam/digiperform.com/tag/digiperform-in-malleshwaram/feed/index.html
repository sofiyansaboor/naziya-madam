]]> BTM, Indiranagar and now in Malleshawaram! Yes, Digiperform is spreading its footprints all over Bangaluru by launching its 3rd center in Malleshwaram, Bengaluru. Bengaluru, formerly known as Bangalore is the capital city of Karnataka with a population of 10 million. This makes it the ultimate megacity and the third most populous city as well as fifth most populous urban agglomeration in India. Bengaluru is known for being the center of India’s high-tech industry. It is one of the most progressive and developed cities which is blessed with a loving and career oriented people. Therefore, Digiperform is geared up to provide a digital career platform to the people of Bengaluru; as it has announced the opening of its 34th center in Bengaluru – Malleshwaram with great pomp and fervor. The launch was followed by great ceremony under the esteemed presence of Digiperform Bengaluru- Malleshwaram Partner, Mohit Gauriar. On this occasion, Mr. Gauriar stated “We decided to expand our footprint in Bangalore after a great response at our Indiranagar Center. We are committed to providing quality education in Digital Marketing and hand on practical learning to all students. We take pride in Digiperform’s course curriculum, our trained industry experts faculty and the fantastic infrastructure of our centers, which promotes great learning experience and functionality during the course. We are located at 8th Cross Malleswaram and well connected to Metro, Bus and other means of transport.”

About Digiperform:

Digiperform India’s Most Trusted Brand in Digital Education who believes to impart digital marketing skills and empower the millions to be the part of the most demanding industries of this century, to all everyone irrespective of their degrees, classes, organization levels, and gender. Digiperform provides two major certification training in online marketing i.e. dCDME and dCDMC to fresh graduates, working professionals, and wannabe entrepreneurs, with a motto to enable them to be self-employed get placed in top organizations & understand the operation of Digital Marketing to run their own empires.
