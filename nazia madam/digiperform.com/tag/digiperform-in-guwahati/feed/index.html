]]> Digiperform is all geared up to spread Digital Marketing to the largest city in the Indian state of Assam which is also the largest urban area in Northeast India. Guwahati is known for its scenic beauty and great climate. Known as ‘Gateway city to India’s Northeast’, Guwahati is the most crowded city and the fastest growing cities in India. With digitalization, it will soon become a digital hub of Northeast India where the demand for digital marketers will definitely increase. Digiperform announced its Guwahati center launch in June with an aim to provide digital marketing training to the people of Guwahati. It is the first ever center in the northeastern region of India. On this auspicious occasion, Arun Jyoti Das (Digiperform Guwahati Partner) quoted, ‘The emergence of digital marketing has led to the reinvention of marketing strategies. As the digital marketing space grows, there is a huge need for skilled workforce in the job market in the digital marketing sector. Right now there is a gap between demand and supply of skilled workforce but DIGIPERFORM is narrowing this gap with their digital marketing courses and excellent content. We are very proud to be associated with DIGIPERFORM.” ABOUT DIGIPERFORM: Digiperform is India’s Leading Digital Marketing Training company that aims to spread its arms all around India and impart online marketing skill training to a million people and help them to achieve the best in their business as well as career. There are multiple of certification training programs offered by Digiperform to fresh graduates, working professionals, and wannabe entrepreneurs, enabling them to be self-employed, get placed in top organizations & understand the operation of Digital Marketing to run their own empires.

The certifications are:

  • Digiperform Certified Digital Marketing Executive Course (dCDME)
  • Digiperform Certified Digital Marketing Consultant Course (dCDMC)
Other than this there are multiple certifications that are offered by different ad tools and platforms that can be added to CVs. The major includes Google Adwords & Analytics Certifications, Bing Ads, Facebook Blueprint Certifications and Multiple Content & Inbound Marketing Certifications offered by Hubspot.
