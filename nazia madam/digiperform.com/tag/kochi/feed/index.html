]]> Digiperform has feathered one more milestone by conducting a 3-Days Corporate Training Workshop in Kochi, Kerala. The workshop was organized with one of the premium outsourcing companies of Kochi – Allianze BPO, which hold clientele across the globe. About Allianze BPO: Allianze BPO International is a leading outsourcing service provider and offers consistent and reliable support services to all the clients placed globally.

How it got started?

In order to globalize on digital platforms, Allianze BPO reached out Digiperorm to seek assistance and training to teach their marketing team on crucial digital marketing domain; like SEM, SMM, Email Marketing, Content Marketing, Website Development, etc. During the Corporate Workshop, The team of Allianze BPO was trained to optimize their global campaigns across digital platforms through live project training. And, guide how these tactics would be cost effective and provide them instant results, which they longed for since the beginning of their tenure. It was our task to conceptualize and develop specialized content to meet the challenges which could help Allianze BPO and the team. The workshop was focussed on individual development where attendees were given the standard tasks to work along with different teams in accordance with specific roles.

The workshop was a huge success….

The 3-days Workshop was a huge success which ended with big cheers and success where Digiperform delivered the desired content as well as exposure to the Team Allianze BPO. The team had been very enthusiastic and curious to learn more and more. Special thanks to wonderful hosts. The training was conducted by the Digiperform’s Head Trainer Mr. Saumya Rajan. The feedback was amazing throughout the workshop and appreciated by wonderfully then expectation.
