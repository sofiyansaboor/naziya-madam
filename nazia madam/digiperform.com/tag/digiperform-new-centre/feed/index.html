We Are Now Live In HSR Layout, Bengaluru!   It’s a happy feeling to announce that we are spreading our wings by launching 5th consecutive center in Bengaluru, HSR Layout as – Digiperform HSR. Our other 4 Bengaluru centers – BTM, Indiranagar, Malleshwaram and Basvanagudi are already running and winning the hearts of Bengalureans; and have empowered 1100+ students with digital marketing training in a span of 8-9 months with 50+ batches still going on. The launch was followed by a great ceremony under the presence of Centre partner of HSR Layout, accompanied by Digiperform’s Co-founder Vaibhav Vats and Chief Operating Officer, Mr. Kunal Virmani.

About the launch

On this auspicious occasion, Digiperform Partner quoted that “At Digiperform, we are committed to providing quality education in Digital Marketing and hand-on practical learning to nurture talents as there is enormous scope to skill people in Tier of 2 & 3 towns of Karnataka. ” Speaking on the launch, Vaibhav Vats, Co-founder, DIGIPERFORM said, “I am pleased to announce that our 4 centres in Bengaluru are already doing and nurturing the talents of Karnataka. With the launch of 5th centre in HSR-Bengaluru, we are motivated to follow the vision of creating a Digital India and creating future talents. Our digital marketing training programs are designed to encourage the spirit of digital innovation in individuals and nurturing them to become the idol who can redefine the world of marketing.”

About Digiperform:

Digiperform India’s Most Trusted Brand in Digital Education who believes to provide digital marketing skills and empower the millions to be the part of the most demanding industries of this century, to all everyone irrespective of their degrees, classes, organization levels, and gender. Digiperform provides these digital marketing courses to fresh graduates, working professionals, and wannabe entrepreneurs, with a motto to enable them to be self-employed get placed in top organizations & understand the operation of Digital Marketing to run their own empires. Any person whether graduates, 12th passed students, housewives, working professionals, and even business owners can join these courses to gain exposure in the digital industry. They can walk-in to the Digiperform HSR center to enroll for the program, wherein they will be provided training live by our well-experienced trainers who are industry experts from various backgrounds sharing their digital marketing knowledge in an immersive and interactive mode, in instructor-led classrooms; with the comfort of accessing the training sessions.
