<b>FAQ: Digital Marketing Course</b>

dCDMC is one umbrella certification in Digital Marketing, Which is the combination of digital marketing fundamentals, Campaign planning & improvement strategy, Organic and Inbound Marketing Modules, Paid marketing Modules and Career Driven Modules.

It’s a combination of about 5 different digital marketing certifications combined into 1 and offering in-depth training in 30 Modules.

Anyone Looking to learn each and every aspect of digital marketing whether a fresher, working professional, business owner or even a freelancer can attend this course.

The candidate must possess basic computer and internet literary skills to efficiently take this program.

We do have flexible batches for working professionals you can choose from early morning, late evening, weekends or Sunday only batches. The schedule and availability depends from centre to centre.

This course has a combined curriculum of 320 hours which involves about 120 hours of classroom training and rest of the hours dedicated towards mandatory assignments, doubt sessions, self study and examinations. For Weekday batches have a Span of about 2 months and weekend 3-4 months.

Each session is divided into 33-50% of class exercises, In addition there are hand-ons assignments that a student completes using over 120 digital marketing tools. To support the student assignments we also provide you with support videos of course content.

In Addition you also get ad budget to run advertising campaign online in every batch to get live campaign management experience.

Digiperform Certification Training is offered at our “Authorised Learning Centres” across India. You can visit the one near you or contact them online to find batch details and join.

You will get dCDMC- “Certified Digital Marketing Consultant Certification” After This Course. With this training you’ll be also able to appear for following Digital Marketing certificates:

  • Digiperform Certified Digital Marketing Consultant Course (dCDME)
  • 5 Google Adwords Certification
  • Google Analytics Certification
  • Shopping Advertising Certification
  • Mobile Advertising Certification
  • 2 Facebook Certification
  • 5 Hubspot Academy Certification
  • Bing Certifications

Majorly both are same. These are two different terms but course content is same. Though theoretically, digital marketing can cover few more concepts of digital mediums like SMS, NFC, Qr codes etc. whereas internet marketing focuses only on internet-based mediums.
